Excellence Is Our Hallmark

Bencon Academy is a respected Ghanaian school offering quality education from Preschool through Basic 6. We believe every child deserves a disciplined, supportive environment in which to learn and thrive.

Our motto, "Excellence Is Our Hallmark," reflects the standard we hold ourselves and our pupils to — in the classroom, on the field, and in daily conduct.

Enrol at Bencon Academy

We welcome applications for all levels from Preschool through Basic 6. Follow the steps below to begin the enrolment process.

1

Contact the School

Call or visit us to confirm available spaces for your child's year group.

2

Collect an Application Form

Pick up an admission form from the school office or request one via email.

3

Submit Required Documents

Birth certificate, passport photo, last school report (if applicable), and completed form.

4

Attend an Assessment

Pupils from Basic 1 and above may be required to complete a brief placement assessment.

5

Receive Confirmation

Once reviewed, you will be notified of acceptance and given details on school fees and start date.

📌 Admissions are currently open for the 2025/2026 academic year. We encourage early application as spaces are limited.
