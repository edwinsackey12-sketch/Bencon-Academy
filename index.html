About Bencon Academy

"We are committed to nurturing well-rounded individuals who are prepared to lead and contribute to their nation and beyond."

Excellence Is Our Hallmark

Bencon Academy is a growing school established in 2021, offering quality education from Preschool through Basic 6. With a dedicated staff of 11 and over 50 pupils, every child enjoys a disciplined and nurturing environment to learn and thrive.

Our motto, "Excellence Is Our Hallmark," reflects the standard we hold ourselves and our pupils to — in the classroom, on the field, and in daily conduct.

Enrol at Bencon Academy

We welcome applications for Preschool and Basic 1 – Basic 6. Follow the simple steps below to begin the enrolment process.

How to Apply
1

Contact the School

Call or visit us to confirm available spaces for your child's year group.

2

Collect an Application Form

Pick up an admission form from the school office or request one via email.

3

Submit Required Documents

Birth certificate, passport photo, last school report (if applicable), and completed form.

4

Attend an Assessment

Pupils from Basic 1 and above may be required to complete a brief placement assessment.

5

Receive Confirmation

You will be notified of acceptance and given details on school fees and start date.

📌 Admission in Progress! We are actively enrolling pupils for the 2025/2026 academic year. Contact us today — spaces are limited!
